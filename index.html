WaDi: Weight Direction-aware Distillation for One-step Image Synthesis

Lei Wang, Yang Cheng, Senmao Li, Ge Wu, Yaxing Wang#, Jian Yang#

CVPR, 2026

Paper BibTeX Project Page Code
Key idea: We propose the Low-rank Rotation of Weight Direction (LoRaD), a parameter-efficient adapter tailored to one-step diffusion distillation. LoRaD models structured directional changes through learnable low-rank rotation matrices and is integrated into Variational Score Distillation (VSD), resulting in Weight Direction-aware Distillation (WaDi).
